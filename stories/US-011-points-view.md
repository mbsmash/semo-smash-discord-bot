# US-011: View Scores

## Summary

As a community member, I want to be able to quickly see the current season's scores, so I can be an engaged and super enthusiastic good noodle.

## Acceptance Criteria

**Given** that teams, scores, and players exist in the database

**When** a community member uses the command `/points` with no arguments or additional parameters

**Then** the bot responds with a list of teams and their scores

**And** below the list are the two top scorers for each team in points.
